About Annual Supercruise

Annual Supercruise Festival

The Annual Supercruise Event currently dubbed as Capital Fest is a festival or a celebration done annually on the first month of each year. the celebration takes a period of about 7 days. People from different states aree usually welcome to attend the event as they get to enjoy music, comedy, booze, gospel, old-school hip-hop, and house music!. The annual Supercruise is usually not celebrated in a fixed location each year. The venue of the event is usually selected randomly and in advance.

History of the Annual SuperCruise Festival

The Annual Supercruise festivals began way back in 2010. The main objective behind the festivity was to promote culture and celebrate life!
